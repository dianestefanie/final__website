What is the website all about?

The website is all about creating a simple portal for SHS students. It will provide access to announcements, class schedules, and downloadable study resources. It will also allow students to contact teachers or the school office. The portal aims to improve communication between students, teachers and the school administration.

Project Goals

Provide Accessible Information

Create an easy-to-use portal where SHS students can find recipes, cooking tips and resources.

Promote Practical Learning

Offer step-by-step guides to practice cooking skills and kitchen safety.

Encourage Healthy Habits

Introduce simple and budget-friendly meals for better lifestyle.

Problems

1. Technical Skills – Students may lack knowledge in web development.

2. Limited Resources – Internet and devices may be limited.

3. Time Management – Balancing school and project is hard.

4. Content Quality – Making good content is challenging.

5. Design and Usability – Needs effort to make it user-friendly.

6. Collaboration Issues – Group coordination can be difficult.

7. Sustainability – Maintaining the portal is challenging.
